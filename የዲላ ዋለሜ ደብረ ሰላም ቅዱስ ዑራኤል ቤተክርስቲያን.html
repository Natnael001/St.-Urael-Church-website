About Our Church

Church History

የዲላ ዋለሜ ደብረ ሰላም ቅዱስ ዑራኤል ቤተክርስቲያን is a sacred place of worship that has served the Orthodox faithful for many years. Our church stands as a beacon of faith, hope, and community in the heart of Dila Waleme.

Built with the dedication and sacrifice of our community members, this holy place continues the ancient traditions of Ethiopian Orthodox Christianity, providing spiritual guidance and fellowship to all who enter its doors.

About St. Urael

St. Urael (ቅዱስ ዑራኤል) is one of the seven archangels in Ethiopian Orthodox tradition, known as the "Fire of God" or "Light of God." He is revered as a powerful intercessor and protector of the faithful.

Our church is dedicated to St. Urael because of his role as a guardian and guide for those seeking spiritual enlightenment and divine protection. Through his intercession, we seek God's blessing and guidance in our daily lives.
